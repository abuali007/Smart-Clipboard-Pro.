Smart Clipboard Pro Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 13, 2025

Smart Clipboard Pro stores your clipboard history locally on your device so you can quickly reuse important snippets. Your data never leaves your browser unless you explicitly export it.

Information We Collect

  • Clipboard data: Stored locally using chrome.storage.local. We never transmit clipboard contents to remote servers.
  • Extension settings: Theme, preferences, and license status are saved locally so the experience is consistent.
  • License verification: When you activate Pro, the extension contacts the license server (Lemon Squeezy) to verify your key. Only the license key and product identifiers are sent over HTTPS.

How Data Is Used

  • Clipboard history is used to populate the in-extension history, pinned items, snippets, and deleted items recovery.
  • Usage analytics (copy counts, characters pasted) are stored locally to show productivity insights.
  • License information is used to determine whether Pro features should be enabled.

Data Control

  • You can export, import, or clear all stored data from Settings → Data Management.
  • Deleted items remain in the recovery list for up to 30 days and can be restored or permanently removed at any time.
  • Uninstalling the extension removes all locally stored information.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy or need to request manual data deletion, please email litextools@gmail.com.
